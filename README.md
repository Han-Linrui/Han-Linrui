# Hi there, I'm Linrui Han 👋

> *"Generative models learn statistical distributions; they don't learn Newton's laws. The path to true Spatial Intelligence requires deterministic grounding."*

I am an undergraduate at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)**, majoring in Data Science. My academic journey bridges rigorous engineering foundations (C++ / Data Structures) with system-level analytical thinking. 

Currently, my research focuses on the intersection of **Reinforcement Learning (RL)** and **Multimodal World Models**. I am passionate about solving "spatio-temporal hallucinations" in diffusion architectures (e.g., DiT) by introducing **Simulation-in-the-Loop (SITL)** mechanisms—leveraging deterministic physics engines (like Unreal Engine 5) as the ultimate ground-truth reward for AI alignment.

### 🔬 Research Interests
- **Multimodal RL Alignment:** Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO), PPO, and Multi-Armed Bandits (MAB) for latent space reasoning.
- **Simulation-in-the-Loop:** Bridging probabilistic generative models with deterministic game engines (UE5/Chaos Physics).
- **Physical Consistency:** Moving beyond human-preference RLAIF towards verifiable physics-informed rewards (RLVR).

### 🚀 Featured Project
-[**LOLA-RL-Alignment**](https://github.com/Han-Linrui/LOLA-Algorithm-Reproduction-Analysis)
  A mechanistic reproduction of the `LLM-2UCB` algorithm (Marketing Science, 2025). This project demonstrates how Multi-Armed Bandit (MAB) online feedback can mathematically bound and correct the static hallucinations of Large Language Models. *This serves as my algorithmic foundation for future research in multimodal latent space exploration.*
